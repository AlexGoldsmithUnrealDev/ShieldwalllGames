Who We Are

History Is Our Game

Shieldwall Games was built on a single conviction: the past is not a backdrop — it is the story. We are historians, designers, and players who believe that the greatest tales ever told are the ones that actually happened.

Every game we make is grounded in primary sources, shaped by genuine research, and designed to put you inside the decisions that changed the world. We don't simplify history — we make it playable.

From the shield walls of the early medieval world to the courts and campaigns that defined civilisations, our games are made for people who want to feel the weight of the past in every move they make.
