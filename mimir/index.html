Yes. The Free tier includes the full desktop application and all worldbuilding features. AI features (Ask Mimir, translation, MCP server) work on Free if you bring your own API key from Anthropic, OpenAI, or another provider. The paid tiers include managed AI access so you don't need your own key.
MCP stands for Model Context Protocol — an open standard that lets AI tools read structured data from external sources. Mimir's MCP server gives Claude, Cursor, and other AI tools direct access to your world data. That means your AI assistant knows your characters, locations, and lore without you having to paste it in every session.
Because Mimir exports to plain markdown and structured JSON, it works with any engine that can read files — Unreal Engine, Unity, Godot, and others. We are building dedicated integrations for Unreal and Godot as priority targets, with Unity to follow.
Primarily indie game developers who are also worldbuilders — people building original IP and needing their lore to be as useful to their tools as it is to their imagination.
