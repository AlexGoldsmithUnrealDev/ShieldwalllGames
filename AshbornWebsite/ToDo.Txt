ASHBORN: FIRE & BLOOD
Brutal. Tactical. Relentless. This is Viking combat redefined.

Ashborn: Fire & Blood is a squad-based, PvE action game that drops you into the heart of real-time melee warfare. Built in Unreal Engine 5.6, it’s a tactical raid experience where you don’t just fight — you lead. Every raid is a high-stakes operation demanding formation mastery, precision execution, and ruthless decision-making.

KEY FEATURES
1. Squad-Based Tactical Combat
Command a warband of AI-controlled companions in tight, formation-based combat.

Issue instant commands for Shieldwall, Wedge, Loose, or Circle formations with seamless input.

Your warband isn't decoration — they’re your survival strategy.

2. Real-Time Melee with Weight and Consequence
Perform animation-driven light and heavy attacks, dodges, parries, and executions.

Combat uses collision and trace-based hit detection with feedback-heavy reactions and stagger systems.

Manage positioning, attack angles, and enemy threat levels for maximum efficiency.

3. Adaptive AI Enemies
Enemies react to your formations, break your lines, and flank your position.

AI Leaders boost nearby troops and respond dynamically to player aggression.

Expect counters, not cannon fodder — every enemy is a puzzle, not a punching bag.

4. Raid. Extract. Evolve.
Missions are multi-objective tactical raids: clear enemies, hold objectives, retrieve loot, and extract alive.

Choose your approach: coordinated, defensive progression or aggressive high-risk pushes.

Failing to extract means losing resources. Survive to grow stronger.

5. No Inventory Mid-Raid – All About Momentum
Loot and rewards are earned post-match. No in-match inventory micromanagement.

Focus on tactics, positioning, and combat — not spreadsheets.

6. Progression-Driven Camp System
Upgrade your base with supplies and silver from successful raids.

Unlock training grounds, feasting halls, smithies, shrines, and more.

Every structure boosts combat performance, morale, or long-term progression.

7. Companions with Purpose
Recruit and level AI heroes with unique traits, weapon styles, and tactical roles.

Heroes participate in formations, deal damage, hold lines, and even respond to morale systems.

No filler characters — every companion brings strategic depth.

8. Modular Loadouts and Unlock Systems
Mix and match weapons, armor, and abilities for your warlord and heroes.

Unlock higher tiers of gear by upgrading infrastructure — or by daring harder raids.

Specialize your builds to suit your raiding style: tank, damage dealer, disruptor, support.

9. Scalable Co-op Multiplayer
Designed for 1–4 player co-op with host-controlled session structure.

Vacant slots are filled with your leveled companions — every raid is full-scale, always.

Commands, progression, and formation behavior work seamlessly across multiplayer.

10. Endless Replayability
Procedural objectives and escalating enemy difficulty keep raids fresh.

Unlock and activate Boasts: high-risk, high-reward challenges that offer silver, supplies, and rare upgrades.

Modifiers and difficulty tiers push elite players to their limits.

WHY ASHBORN STANDS APART
No button-mashing. No passive AI. No corridor brawls.

This is strategic melee combat — every second matters, every decision has weight.

Designed for players who want control, challenge, and real progression.

Ashborn: Fire & Blood is not about grinding or gimmicks. It’s about tactical excellence, warband mastery, and the relentless pursuit of combat perfection.
