About Shieldwall Games

A studio built on a single belief: history is the greatest story ever told.

Shieldwall Games was founded by people who spent their childhoods reading sagas, studying battlefields, and wondering what it truly felt like to stand in a shield wall.

We believe the best games don't invent worlds — they reveal them. Our mission is to craft experiences that honour the complexity, brutality, and beauty of real history, brought to life with modern design and deep, rewarding gameplay.
