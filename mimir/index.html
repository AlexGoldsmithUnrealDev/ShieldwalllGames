Everything in one place

One world. Every tool. Infinite stories.

Mimir is a desktop application for structured worldbuilding. Your entries live as plain markdown files — readable by humans, ready for AI, and native to any game engine that can read text.

MCP Server & Game Engine Integration

From your world
to your engine, instantly.

Mimir's MCP server connects your world data directly to Claude, Cursor, and other AI tools — so your assistant always knows your lore. Your entries also export as structured data ready for Unreal, Unity, or Godot.
