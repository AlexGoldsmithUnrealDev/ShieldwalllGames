The Game

History is the battlefield.

Ashborn CCG is a collectible card game set in the Viking Age — not the mythology, but the history. The raids. The halls. The brutal calculus of war and alliance.

Each deck is a warband with its own character. Each match is a saga in miniature — decided by tactics, timing, and the cards fate puts in your hand.

Designed for players who want depth without doctrine, and flavour grounded in the real world.

The World

One world. Many stories.

Every Ashborn game is set in the same Viking Age world — the same coastlines, the same halls, the same feuds playing out across different formats.

Built on History

Ashborn is not a fantasy setting dressed in Viking clothing. The world draws directly from the historical record — the material culture, the social structures, the violence and the poetry of the early medieval North.

Built with Mimir

The Ashborn world is being built inside Mimir — our own worldbuilding tool. Every character, location, and faction exists as structured data that feeds directly into the game's design.
