Forging Legends, One Saga at a Time

At Shieldwall, we craft Viking-inspired games that echo with the thunder of ancient battles and the spirit of the North.

Who We Are

Shieldwall is an independent game studio devoted to bringing Norse myth and Viking history to life through immersive gameplay and rich storytelling. Founded by passionate developers and historians, we believe in building games that inspire awe, strategy, and bravery.

Shieldwall Games Ltd – Our Story

Shieldwall Games was not born in a boardroom. There were no investors, no publishers, no venture capital. There was no office, no staff, no formal training. Just four friends — tired of waiting for someone else to make the kind of game we wanted to play — and one burning idea in the mind of a storyteller named Alex.

At the beginning, we were outsiders. None of us had a game dev degree. None of us worked at a major studio... Some people call it naive. We call it necessary.

A Story of Rebellion

The story of Shieldwall Games is a story of rebellion — against an industry that too often puts profit above passion, that demands crunch instead of creativity... So we built something outside it.

We started with Ashborn: Fire & Blood, our first game and the opening chapter of a much larger saga — one that spans centuries, continents, and mythologies...

Our Dream

Shieldwall Games isn’t just a studio. It’s a philosophy — that games should be made by people who live and breathe them...

We’re building toward a future where we can operate from a self-sustaining, eco-conscious studio...

Our Values

  • No Publishers. Ever. No marketing executive will ever override our story...
  • Nature Comes First. We are committed to running our studio sustainably...
  • History Matters. Our fantasy is built on respect and research...
  • Work Should Feel Worthwhile. We will never crunch our team...

How We Get There

We are working in stages. First, completing our vertical slice for Ashborn: Fire & Blood... From there, we’ll launch Ashes as our flagship title...

Shieldwall Games: What We Are

We’re the players who stayed up too late modding medieval games. We’re the writers who filled notebooks with timelines, languages, and lineages. We’re the history nerds who debate theories about Dark Age Britain for fun. We’re the friends who never stopped believing this could be real.

This is just the beginning. Join us — and help us build something worth remembering.
