About

The short version.

I'm a Digital Marketing Manager with a background that doesn't fit neatly into one box. I studied Creative Writing at Edge Hill University (BA Hons, 2:1 — joint with Theatre) — which taught me to tell stories, structure narratives, and care about the reader. Then I applied that to marketing, sales, and building things.

I currently lead digital marketing at Tres Tria Limited, a Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central / Astral 365 reseller — owning the full strategy across SEO, Google Ads, email, content, and conference marketing. I build and maintain the company websites, produce 3D brand assets and promo videos in-house, and manage the social media team. Before that, I spent three years at Saga plc — first as a Customer Advisor (65% extras penetration, 85% retention, Employee of the Year nominee three years running), then as a QA Automation Engineer building front-end skills and leading QA teams.

I've also worked as a freelance Animator & Engineer for Metalyte Pipeworks (Blender, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine), a Teaching Assistant designing educational games for primary school children, and a Hospitality Manager at Port Lympne — where I managed a team of 20 and once hosted David Attenborough.

Since 2020 I've been running Shieldwall Games part-time — an indie studio where I'm building Mimir (a worldbuilding platform in Electron, React, TypeScript) and Ashborn (a Viking Age card game). I recruited and led a dev team using AGILE/SCRUM, and work hands-on across the full pipeline. The portfolio you're reading was built by hand in a single HTML file.

Based in Kent, UK. Open to fully remote roles. Looking for positions where creative range is valued, not penalised.
