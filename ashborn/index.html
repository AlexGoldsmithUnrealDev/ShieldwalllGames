The Game

History is the battlefield.

Ashborn CCG is a collectible card game set in the Viking Age: not the mythology, but the history. The raids. The halls. The brutal calculus of war and alliance.

Each deck is a warband with its own character. Each match is a saga in miniature, decided by tactics, timing, and the cards fate puts in your hand.

Designed for players who want depth without doctrine, and flavour grounded in the real world.

2–4 players. 30–60 minute matches. Starter box with two ready-to-play decks and a full rulebook. Expandable with booster packs and standalone expansions. Designed for kitchen tables, not tournament halls.

Price subject to change before launch. Pre-order and early access details to follow.
